Shipping Policy:

Clients can expect to receive all necessary materials and communications promptly according to the schedule established during the introductory call and subsequent planning stages.

We pride ourselves on the convenience and environmental benefits of our digital service delivery, ensuring that you can start transforming your health immediately and sustainably.
