Shipping Policy

Digital Delivery

At ABD Rajput & Co., all of our services are delivered digitally and involve no physical shipping of products. Our comprehensive transformation plans, coaching sessions, and trackers are all provided online through personalized communications such as WhatsApp, Google Sheets, or direct calls.

Immediate Access

This digital delivery ensures immediate and secure access to all the resources you need for your health journey, regardless of your geographic location.

No Shipping Fees

As there are no physical products shipped, there are no shipping fees or waiting periods associated with the delivery of our services. Clients can expect to receive all necessary materials and communications promptly according to the schedule established during the introductory call and subsequent planning stages.

Environmental Benefits

We pride ourselves on the convenience and environmental benefits of our digital service delivery, ensuring that you can start transforming your health immediately and sustainably.
