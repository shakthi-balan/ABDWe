Privacy Policy

At ABD Rajput & Co., your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose your personal information when you engage with our services.

5.1 Information We Collect

To deliver personalized health and coaching services, we may collect the following information:

Personal details: Name, age, gender, contact number, email address

Health & lifestyle information: Medical history, dietary preferences, fitness levels, habits, sleep patterns, stress levels, blood reports (if shared voluntarily)

Payment information: Transaction confirmations, invoices, and payment references (we do not store card or banking details)

Communication data: Messages, voice notes, and call discussions shared via WhatsApp, email, or other official communication channels

Progress data: Weight, measurements, photos (if voluntarily shared), adherence logs, and tracker entries

Providing this information is voluntary; however, incomplete or inaccurate data may affect the quality and safety of the services provided.

5.2 How We Use Your Information

Your information is collected and used strictly for legitimate business and service purposes, including:

Creating and managing personalized nutrition, training, and lifestyle plans

Monitoring progress and providing coaching feedback

Communicating program updates, reminders, and support

Improving service quality, systems, and internal processes

Fulfilling legal, accounting, and tax obligations where applicable

We do not use your personal data for spam, unauthorized marketing, or unrelated purposes.

5.3 Data Storage and Protection

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

Data is stored digitally using secure tools such as Google Sheets, email systems, and encrypted devices

Access is limited only to authorized team members involved in service delivery

Internal team members are bound by confidentiality obligations

While we follow industry-standard security practices, no digital system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. By using our services, you acknowledge and accept this risk.

5.4 Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Your information may only be shared in the following limited circumstances:

With internal team members strictly for service delivery

When legally required by law, court order, or regulatory authority

With your explicit written consent, if required for any collaboration or referral

5.5 Third-Party Platforms

Our services may be delivered through third-party platforms such as WhatsApp, Google Sheets, Zoom, payment gateways, or email services. While we choose reputable platforms, ABD Rajput & Co. is not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services. Clients are encouraged to review their respective privacy policies.

5.6 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

Deliver the agreed services

Maintain internal records

Comply with legal or tax requirements

After this period, data may be securely deleted or anonymized.

5.7 Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon communication or publication. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

5.8 Consent

By enrolling in our services and sharing your information, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
