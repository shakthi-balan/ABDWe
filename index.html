About Abhyedsingh

Abhyedsingh Rajput, also known as, ABD Rajput, 24, originally from Bagalkot, Karnataka, now based in Delhi, is the founder of "Project Commando," a coaching brand focused on fitness tranformations. His fitness journey began at age 4, influenced by his father's commando training. He excelled in various combat sports and was a professional fighter and footballer during his schooling. Abhyedsingh believes in practicing what he preaches and pushes his own limits to motivate his clients. He started coaching during the Covid-19 pandemic while pursuing his BBA degree and now works full-time building his fitness coaching brand. He has now coached over 1000+ individuals across the globe. His vision is to help busy individuals adopt a fitness lifestyle for lifelong wellness.

Roots

G H Rajput, Abhyedsingh’s father is the foundational pillar of his unwavering fitness journeys and serves as the principal mentor at Project Commando. 56 years old at present, his martial arts and fitness journey began at the age of 30 when he got the opportunity to undergo Commando Training through the Railways. He used to take Abhyedsingh to attend numerous training camps across different cities, Inculcating a disciplined environment from a young age. As a coach, he has imparted invaluable training techniques, fostering Abhyedsingh’s consistent fitness development since childhood. Despite his age, he adheres to a daily training routine with consistency and matches the fitness levels of younger individuals. His unwavering dedication throughout the past 25+ years serves as a prime example at Project Commando.

My Transformation Story

From being Bullied to Coaching them

During my school years, being shorter than my peers brought challenges like bullying and sports team rejection, deeply affecting my confidence. In 9th grade, I faced a pivotal moment : either dwell on my height or take action to build confidence. I chose the latter, focusing on strength training and nutrition changed my life for the good. Though my height didn't change much, my body composition & strength improved, boosting both my physique and confidence. Over time, I became the fittest in my school and college. Now, those who once bullied me seek fitness advice from me, they now want to train & transform under my mentorship. My fitness journey stands as proof of the power of self-improvement rather than dwelling on factors beyond our control.
